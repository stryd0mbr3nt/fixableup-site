Privacy Policy

At FixableUp, your privacy isn’t just respected—it’s protected. We collect only the information we need to help you get trusted local services in Upington, and we never sell or misuse your data.

1. What We Collect

  • Basic contact information (name, email, phone) when you fill out forms
  • Business details if you register as a service provider
  • Optional information to verify provider legitimacy
  • Website usage data (via analytics tools) to improve performance

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Match clients with the right providers
  • Contact you about quotes or jobs
  • Verify service providers
  • Improve and secure our platform

3. Your Data is Yours

You can request access to your data, correction of inaccuracies, or deletion at any time. Just email us at info@fixableup.online.

4. Secure by Default

We use industry-standard protections to keep your data safe. Our website is protected by SSL and hosted on secure infrastructure. Payment data (if applicable) is handled by trusted processors like PayGate or Yoco.

5. Third Parties

We do not sell your information. We only share it with trusted service providers when necessary to fulfill your request—such as emailing quotes to verified providers.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to improve your experience. No tracking cookies from advertisers—only session or functional cookies to support the site’s features.

7. Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We’ll notify you of major changes via email or an announcement on the website.

Effective: 19 May 2025
